intro

Hello world! Thank you for coming to my website.



It has been a pleasure learning to write html, css and all the many tips and tricks that come with it!


With time, practice, patience, and a good internet connection, I believe anyone
can learn to code.

If you're a perfectionist like me, here are some tips to help you with your journey:

  1. Be patient!

    You're going to make lots of mistakes, that's a fact.
    Don't beat yourself up, and remember, Rome wasn't built in a day!


  2. Google, YouTube and Ask away!

    Google search can solve almost any question related to html & css. Don't hesitate to use it! It's been said that many programmers have up to 20 tabs open on any given day! All of them stack overflow, and similar tutorial websites


  3. Ask your teacher!

    if you still can't find the answer, write it down, and come back to it later

    4. If you don't have a teacher, ask around your network, find out who codes in your family, friend circle, your church, anybody!
    nobody said it would be easy, but having a mentor is cruial!
